Military Expenditure by Regions

Military expenditure by regions refers to the amount of money that countries in specific geographic regions or continents allocate for their defense and military-related activities. This information is crucial for assessing regional security dynamics and understanding the distribution of defense resources globally.

The filled bubbles show the amount of military spending by continent in 2022.

North America has the highest military spending, with a budgeted cost of about $900 billion for 2022 alone, most of which is spent by the United States.

The area chart shows the total military expenditures of the five major regions over the past 30 years (1992-2022).

There is an overall increasing trend in all the regions.

Military Expenditure by Countries refers to the financial resources allocated by individual nations to support their defense and military operations. This data is used to analyse a nation's military capabilities, assess its defense priorities, and compare military spending between countries, which can have significant geopolitical implications.
